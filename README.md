# Jan Tauberschmidt

I am a Ph.D. student at RPTU Kaiserslautern and a researcher at the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI), where I work in the Data Science and its Applications group under Prof. Sebastian J. Vollmer.

My research is mainly about machine learning for scientific and physical systems. I am particularly interested in generative modeling, scientific machine learning, physics- and domain-informed AI, and control. Earlier, I worked on reinforcement learning for race driver modeling and spatio-temporal anomaly detection.

I received my M.Sc. in Mathematics from RPTU Kaiserslautern in 2024, specializing in systems and control.
